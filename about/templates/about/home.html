What is LaunchLink?

We are LaunchLink, a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering high school students and small businesses in our community through technology. At LaunchLink, we believe in the transformative power of technology and the potential it holds for inclusive growth. Together, we are shaping a brighter future for our community, one empowered small business at a time.







How Does it Work?

Our passionate team of students collaborates with local small businesses to create personalized websites to help them establish a strong online presence and reach a wider audience.







How Can I Join?

To begin, signup and answer a few short questions. Once you have created your account, it will be verified by our team within 5 business days. Once your account has been verified, businesses may request for the creation or projects and students may sign up for a project they would like to work on.
